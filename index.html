WHAT IS A NANOPUBLICATION?

Your research, in building blocks others can build on

A nanopublication captures one clear piece of your research — a finding, a dataset, a replication result — in a format that's citable, searchable, and permanently yours. No more invisible work buried in PDFs.

For Universities & Research Institutes

Meet funder mandates. Turn research into impact.

Funders demand FAIR data and measurable impact. But too much research stays locked in PDFs that industry and society never see. Science Live makes your researchers' contributions structured, discoverable, and ready to be used by the people who need them.

FAIR compliance, automatic

Horizon Europe, NIH, NSF, and most national funders now mandate FAIR data plans. Every nanopublication is FAIR by design — demonstrate compliance without extra documentation.

From research to impact

Make findings discoverable by industry, policymakers, and other researchers. Build the impact case studies funders increasingly require — backed by real, traceable contributions.

Research output reporting

Track structured contributions across departments — datasets, methods, replications, claims. Generate reports for REF, accreditation, and internal evaluations in minutes, not months.

Visibility for RSEs and research engineers

Make hard-to-justify staff contributions measurable and discoverable. Show funders and industry what your research engineering team actually delivers.
