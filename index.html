WHAT IS A NANOPUBLICATION?

Your research, in building blocks others can build on

A nanopublication captures one clear piece of your research — a finding, a dataset, a replication result — in a format that's citable, searchable, and permanently yours. No more invisible work buried in PDFs.

A scientific claim (AIDA sentence) — one of many nanopublication types you can create

FOR RESEARCHERS

Make your invisible work visible

Literature annotations, citation assessments, replication studies, PRISMA reviews — all become published, citable contributions linked to your ORCID.

Permanent attribution

Every nanopub gets a persistent URI and your ORCID cryptographic signature. Your work follows you across institutions.

Zotero integration

Create nanopublications directly from your Zotero library — annotate papers, declare citations, publish claims.

PRISMA workflows

Conduct structured systematic reviews. Each step — search strategy, screening, assessment — becomes a traceable nanopub.
